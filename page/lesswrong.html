Less Wrong is an online community which was created with the goal of sharing and developing 'the art of human Rationality'. %%note: This includes self-improvement techniques, the habit of to gradually updating in response to evidence, awareness of [cognitive_biases biases] (e.g. conjunction fallacy, the affect heuristic, and scope insensitivity), and many other things.%% Common topics include cognitive biases, philosophy, psychology, economics, rationality, decision theory, neuroscience, and artificial intelligence.

The sequences

The original sequences, LessWrong's core content, was written by Eliezer Yudkowsky. MIRI has since collated and edited the sequences into [rationality_a_to_z Rationality: From AI to Zombies book].
