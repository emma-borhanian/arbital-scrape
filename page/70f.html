Is this what is meant by transitive and nontransitive set?

Transitive:

$~$A = \{ \{ 1,2 \}, \{ 3,4 \}, 1, 2, 3, 4 \}$~$

$~$x = \{1,2\}$~$

$~$a = 2$~$

$~$a \in x$~$, $~$x \in A$~$ and $~$a \in A$~$

Nontransitive:

$~$B = \{ \{ 1,2 \}, \{ 3,4 \} \}$~$

$~$y = \{1,2\}$~$

$~$b = 2$~$

$~$b \in y$~$, $~$y \in B$~$ but $~$b \notin B$~$
