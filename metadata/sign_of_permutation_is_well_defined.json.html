shorthand for $(12)(3)(4)\\dots(n-1)(n)$.\nWe claim that multiplying $\\sigma$ by a transposition either increases $c(\\sigma)$ by $1$, or decreases it by $1$.\n\nIndeed, let $\\tau = (kl)$.\nEither
