There is an open question of whether or not The plan experiment should be public. My current intuition is that as much as possible should be public.

For:

  • Creates opportunity for common knowledge and simplifying the social dynamic
  • Makes it easier to create individual plans
  • Creates an atmosphere of openness and mutual trust
  • Allows for wider collaboration

Against:

  • Some people might panic
