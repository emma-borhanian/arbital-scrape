An unlisted page is a page that's not part of any domain. It can be submitted to a domain by its creator or a domain member. An unlisted page doesn't show up in search results for anyone except the creator of the page.

Because unlisted pages haven't been reviewed by Arbital experts, they carry no assurance of quality. Read them at your own peril.
