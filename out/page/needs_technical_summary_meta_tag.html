Pages about concepts which can be described in terse domain-specific jargon or notation should have a technical summary for people familiar with it. These show up as an alternate tab on the summary popover when a user hovers over a greenlink. Add this tag to pages that need to have a technical summary.

Pages may also require a general or brief summary.
